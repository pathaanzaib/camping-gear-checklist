# Camping Gear Checklist

This is a simple and practical camping gear checklist for beginners and outdoor enthusiasts.

## Essentials
- Tent
- Sleeping bag
- Backpack
- Cooking gear
- Water and food supplies

## Tips
- Always check weather conditions before your trip
- Keep your gear lightweight but durable
- Plan according to your camping location
